Discover the wonders of our Exhibition Spaces

Cosmology

The Sky Above Us

Explore the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We'll locate the various constellations and galexies that can be seen.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn't survive. including our Wooly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Accessibility:

Our museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braile display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink:

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks, and more.

Shop:

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the Museum.
