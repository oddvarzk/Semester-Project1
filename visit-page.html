Planning your visit

Museums are wonderful places full of exciting things to see and learn about. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned museum-goer, planning your visit ahead of time can help you make the most of your experience.

You may have some questions about what to expect when you go to a museum. What will you see? What will you learn? These are all great questions, and we're here to help you answer them.

In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know to plan a fun and educational visit to a museum. From choosing the right museum to exploring the exhibits.

Admission:

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.
